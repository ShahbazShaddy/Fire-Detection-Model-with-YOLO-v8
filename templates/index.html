Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the fire detection?

Our system has a 95% detection accuracy with less than 0.5% false positives in standard environments. Performance may vary based on specific conditions.

Can I use my existing cameras?

Yes, FireGuard AI works with most standard security cameras. For optimal performance, we recommend cameras with at least 720p resolution.

How is data privacy maintained?

All video processing occurs locally or on secure cloud infrastructure. We maintain strict data handling protocols and comply with relevant privacy regulations.

What happens during power outages?

Our enterprise systems include battery backup options and can reconnect automatically when power is restored.
